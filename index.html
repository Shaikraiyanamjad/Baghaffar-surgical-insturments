Our Services

Providing Complete Diagnostic & Laboratory Solutions Across the Kingdom

Baghaffar Sons Co. specializes in supplying advanced diagnostic equipment, laboratory instruments, and research-grade materials. With over 30 years of experience, we ensure every laboratory — big or small — is equipped with reliable, cost-effective, and modern solutions.

Diagnostic Equipment Supply

Comprehensive range of diagnostic instruments and analyzers sourced from international manufacturers to meet clinical and research demands.

Laboratory Instruments

From microscopes to centrifuges and automation systems, we supply modern tools essential for efficient and accurate laboratory operations.

Technical Support & Maintenance

Our expert service team works round the clock to provide installation, calibration, and ongoing technical support for uninterrupted lab performance.

Research & Specialized Products

Providing advanced solutions for research laboratories, including specialized reagents, instruments, and test kits for experimental applications.

Shipping & Delivery

Efficient nationwide delivery handled by our shipping department, ensuring products reach clients safely and on schedule anywhere in the Kingdom.

Small Laboratory Solutions

We offer affordable, space-saving instruments ideal for smaller labs with fewer samples, helping reduce costs without compromising quality.

Product Consultation

Expert guidance in selecting diagnostic systems and laboratory setups that perfectly fit your testing needs and workflow.

After-Sales Support

Comprehensive post-purchase assistance including spare parts, upgrades, and performance checks to ensure long-term reliability.
